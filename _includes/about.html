What is Africa Muslim Fest?

Africa Muslim Fest is not a single event but an ecosystem of communities, events, and organisations that will converge in Cape Town, South Africa.

The city will host the first-ever Africa Muslim Fest — a celebration of Muslim innovation, culture, and futures rooted in our tradition.

  • Cape Town comes alive with the first-ever festival celebrating Muslim innovation, culture, and futures.
  • One shared experience that spans tech, culture, wellness, business, social impact, and spiritual programmes.
  • Designed together so every partner adds their voice to the Africa Muslim Fest story.

A festival beyond a single stage

Across the city, experiences interconnect to showcase the breadth of Muslim excellence — from emerging tech leaders to cultural visionaries and wellness practitioners.

Powered by collaboration

Rather than one organiser controlling the programme, we convene many partners. Each event keeps its identity while contributing to the wider Africa Muslim Fest experience.
